Website Evaluations

Canva.com

Canva is an online graphic design platform where users can make visual content. It's a versatile platform for just about any visual media like posters, logos, and presentations

  • Contrast: A lot of elements stand out. Each icon has pleasing colors, the font looks nice, text is very easy to read with not to many words, and while using black on white color for text, doesn't feel offensive at all
  • Repetition: It's consistent in all the elements it displays, even in other pages. Words are wrapped and aligned the same in all places
  • Alignment: Very neatly spaced with not too much empty space but not filled to the brim with text or links.
  • Proximity: Everything feels very effective. Things are group where they should and make it convenient for both new and old users. Something I really like is home page shows what you can make, then you can click on what you want for a template all while not having to scroll or look for it.
